<h4>:: VoceAnatomy ::</h4>


<center>
<img src="/assets/images/res/skel1.jpg" class="img-responsive" width="30%" style="float: center" />
</center>

Voce-Anatomy is a real-time, hands-free educational tool for exploring a high-resolution, open-source 3D model of human anatomy. Instead of a mouseand keyboard, you speak commands — "show the heart," "rotate," "zoom in 20 percent" — and point and pinch in the air to select and manipulate structures, using nothing but a microphone and a webcam. It's designed for classroom and demo settings where a presenter can navigate the body naturally, without breaking eye contact with an audience to click through menus.
