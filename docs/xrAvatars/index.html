See Related Papers and Related Projects:
AI Modeling Thought & Language   AI Cognitive Creativity
AI Affective Virtual Human   Sensing Humans (Bio/Brain/Face/Movement/VR)

Researchers: Steve DiPaola, Mahdi Davoodikakhki, Andrey Goncharov, Nilay Ozge Yalcin

The Research XR (VR/AR/3D) systems where a person (at home or work) can fully express themselves as a 3D avatar in desktop or full VR. With and without motion tracking their gestures and facial expressions. For education (fully credited university XR class), as coaches for the elderly and others, and as a trainer in health (like we did here with nurses during covid).
